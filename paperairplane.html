How to make a paper airplane

Steps

See video

Steps

  1. Fold the paper vertically.
      aka hotdog fold
  2. Bring the top corners toward the crease in the middle.
      make sure the crease is up and down, so the folds line up
  3. Fold the top down so there is 1 inch left on the bottom.
      don't fold all the way down to the edge of the paper
  4. Bring the top corners toward the center.
      fold each side one at a time
  5. Hold the folds in place by folding the bottom point over them.
      make sure the fold holds the triangles in place
  6. Fold the paper in half (opposite way of the original fold) along the center crease.
      make sure you can see your previous folds on the outside of the plane
  7. Crease the wings down so they line up on the bottom edge.
      make sure the wings are the same size, so the plane properly flys
